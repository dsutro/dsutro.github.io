Musical Journey

My love for music began during my time at UC Santa Cruz, where I fell in love with all things music. Whether I'm in the audience or on the stage, music is a central passion in my life.

I play guitar and focus on combining my many influences from blues, funk, latin, and jazz. This eclectic mix of styles allows me to explore different musical landscapes and create unique sounds that reflect my diverse musical interests.

During my senior year at UC Santa Cruz, I completed multiple capstone projects that combined music and artificial intelligence, exploring the intersection of technology and creativity. These projects allowed me to merge my two passions and discover new ways that technology can enhance and interact with musical expression.
