About me

I'm a software engineer with a passion for data science, artificial intelligence, and machine learning. My educational background in embedded engineering and machine learning has shaped my approach to solving complex technical challenges in both enterprise and project settings.

Professionally, I have extensive experience with FAA DO-178C certification processes, software verification test infrastructure, automation, Python, Databricks, SQL, and Docker. I enjoy being proactive in finding opportunities to enable those I work with to be more efficient.

Most of my professional career has been at Joby Aviation, where I've grown from an intern (employee #364) to a full-time engineer, experiencing firsthand the transformation from a small agile startup to a multi-billion dollar company with operations across the globe.

Beyond engineering, I'm a passionate musician. I play guitar and focus on combining influences from blues, funk, latin, and jazz. During my time at UC Santa Cruz, I fell in love with music and even completed multiple senior capstone projects involving music and artificial intelligence.
